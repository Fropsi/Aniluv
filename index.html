Get to know me

I'm Aniluv! In 2019, before the pandemic, I was a member of a dance crew known as Gut It Out (GIO). I also received training from Alive One, a group that recently participated in a dance competition in Australia in September 2023. Currently, I'm part of the PLV Dance Company at my school. I regularly attend dance classes taught by exceptional instructors in Valenzuela and have established connections with talented dancers and groups in our city. I'm confident that I can assist you in learning to dance if you're a beginner or in improving your dance skills.

Join us at Dance Class 2023 and become part of a vibrant dance community that celebrates the beauty of movement.

Discover the magic of dance with us today!
